Reproductive & perinatal mental health.

Solo private practice in San Francisco + Marin, providing therapy for individuals navigating infertility, pregnancy, postpartum, and family building. Telehealth available across California.

My approach

A relational, evidence-based practice.

I work from an integrative clinical style that brings together mindfulness-based cognitive therapy, relational theory, and psychodynamic understanding. The therapeutic relationship is the foundation; the modalities are tools in service of your goals.

Relational

Genuine connection and collaboration. I believe strongly in the inner wisdom within each of us; at times we just need additional support.

Integrative

Mindfulness-based cognitive therapy with relational and psychodynamic foundations, applied to the specific context of reproductive mental health.

Specialty-driven

Postgraduate certificate training in infertility counseling (ASRM) and perinatal mood and anxiety disorders (PSI) — credentials most Bay Area therapists don't have.

Specialties

Areas of focus.

A few of the most common reasons people reach out. If your situation isn't listed, please reach out — I likely work with it.

Infertility & family building

Coping with the stress of infertility, navigating treatment options, support through IUI/IVF, secondary infertility, third-party family building, donor conception, and the childfree decision.

Pregnancy & postpartum

Adjustment to pregnancy, perinatal mood and anxiety disorders, postpartum support, pregnancy loss, and the early-parenthood transition.

Reproductive mental health

Cancer in young women and fertility preservation, donor-conceived individuals, former donors connecting with genetic offspring, and the sandwich generation of caring for children and aging parents.

Women's mental health

Mid-life transitions, loss and grief, life transitions, mood and anxiety challenges, and relationship issues across the reproductive lifespan.
