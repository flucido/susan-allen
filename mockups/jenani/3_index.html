My Practice

Women's reproductive, perinatal and maternal mental health

My practice focus is women's reproductive, perinatal and maternal mental health. I provide individual therapy for infertility, pregnancy and postpartum, third-party family building, women at mid-life, and parenting kids of any age in any family structure. I also work with donor conceived individuals, and with former donors who want to meaningfully connect with genetic offspring.

My general practice areas include loss and grief, life transitions, understanding and managing mood and anxiety challenges, sandwich generation stress, and relationship issues. I work with clients in the San Francisco Bay Area.

I believe strongly in the inner wisdom within each of us; at times we just need additional support. Working from a relational approach, I encourage genuine connection and collaboration between us. My clinical style integrates mindfulness-based cognitive therapy with relational and psychodynamic theories. I love the transformational nature of this work and believe in our capacity for change and growth. Therapy can be an enriching experience that brings about lasting change. I encourage you to reach out for support.

Therapy Process

Developing a deeper understanding of ourselves

Therapy involves examining our lives to develop a deeper understanding of ourselves and what holds us back from change. My role is to encourage self-awareness and help create a conscious shift toward positive changes in your life. Together we will explore patterns that may be impacting your life and relationships so you can see beyond limitations and experience more balance and connection. It is my hope that our work together will support you in feeling less stressed and more optimistic about what you can bring to your life.

Practice Focus

There are many life changes during the reproductive years

That impact one's psychological / emotional health, identity and relationships. My practice focuses on maternal and reproductive mental health, new parenthood and parenting across the lifespan.

Infertility and Reproductive Health
Coping with the stress of infertility, impact on relationships, exploring treatment options, support through IUI/IVF, secondary infertility, cancer in young women and fertility preservation, childfree decision-making, peri/menopause transition.
Pregnancy and Postpartum
Decision-making around having children, pregnancy emotional health, high risk pregnancy, postpartum anxiety and depression, TFMR decision-making, pregnancy loss, birth trauma, preterm birth / NICU stays, new parent self-care, parenting kids.
Family Building with the Help of a Donor
Third party family building via donor egg, sperm or embryo, or gestational carrier. Talking openly with children about donor conception, creating / sharing your family story, genetic relatedness, support for donor conceived individuals.
General Practice
Loss and grief, life transitions, understanding and managing mood and anxiety challenges, sandwich generation stress, relationship issues.

Logistics & Finances

Working together

Please contact me to discuss my current fee and availability. Payment is due at each session, payable by electronic payment.

Do you accept insurance? +

My practice is self-pay. I am an out of network provider. I do not work directly with any insurance carriers. For those with a PPO plan, you may be eligible to receive a partial reimbursement depending on your plan's out of network benefit. Please contact your provider.

What is your cancellation policy? +

Appointment cancellations made with less than 24 hours advance notice will result in a full session fee charge.

Do you do RE-required intended-parent consultations? +

No. If your RE referred you for a required intended parent psychoeducational consultation meeting, I don't offer those meetings. I provide ongoing psychotherapy and reproductive counseling. Please refer back to your clinic.

Are you licensed in California? +

I am licensed in California and only work with California-based clients. For information related to Good Faith Estimate of treatment costs, please see Notice of Right to Receive Good Faith Estimate.

Telehealth or in person? +

Both. In-person sessions in San Francisco and Marin; telehealth across California.
