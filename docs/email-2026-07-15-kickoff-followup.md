### 1. What we found on the audit (the short version)

Walking your site through our sensory / emotional / meaning framework, four things stand out as the most worth fixing in the template refresh:

- **The "Services" page silently redirects to home.** You probably noticed this isn't ideal — anyone who types `susanallentherapy.com/services` (or follows an old link) just lands on your home page with no indication they didn't get what they asked for. We'll rebuild this as a real "Specialties" page.
- **Your H1 (the largest text on every page) is your brand name.** So when someone lands on the About page, the biggest thing they see is "SUSAN ALLEN LMFT | PSYCHOTHERAPY" — not the page content. We'll fix this so the H1 = the page topic, brand stays in the header.
- **Color contrast doesn't pass WCAG AA on links/backgrounds.** This is a real issue (it's moving from "recommendation" to "ADA compliance") and it's also why some AI/chat bots won't index your site. Quick fix in the template config.
- **Your ASRM / PSI / Donor Conceived Community credentials are buried in body text.** These are the single biggest differentiator you have — most Bay Area therapists don't have this depth. We're going to surface them visually the way they deserve.

The full audit (with all 6 gates) is attached if you want to see more.
