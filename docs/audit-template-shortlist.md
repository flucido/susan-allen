# Susan Allen — Squarespace Template Shortlist

> Shortlist of 4 Squarespace templates from the **Health & Beauty** category that fit "warm/specialty health practices." Selected 7/13 against the WFC 6-gate framework + Susan's specific specialty (reproductive + perinatal mental health). Each goes through a full audit in [[template-audits]].

## The 22 health/beauty templates surveyed

From https://www.squarespace.com/templates/browse/topic/health-beauty/ (22 total, 7.1-era):

Aurora · Jenani · Anza · Looped · Fletcher · Achromatic · Kusa · Meriden · Florence · Almar · Randi · Myhra · Altaloma · Saltless · Emmeline · Maca · Alignflow · Lakshi · Clune · Colima · idrah · Clove

## Why these 4

Susan's site needs (per [[leads/wfc-susan-allen/dossier#WFC-Fit-Analysis]] + [[leads/wfc-susan-allen/SOW-2026-07-07]]):

1. **Specialty-prioritized** — ASRM/PSI credentials prominent (her biggest SEO differentiator)
2. **Warm, not sterile** — reproductive + perinatal = users are often anxious, overwhelmed
3. **Services section** — currently missing; "/services" silently redirects to home
4. **About / team showcase** — credentialed practitioner pages
5. **Contact + intake-friendly** — even though intake stays in email, the path to contact must be clear
6. **Calm color palette** — WFC Calm Base target: peachy cream + sage green
7. **Mobile responsive** — many prospective clients browse on phones during a moment of stress

## Templates I deliberately didn't include (and why)

- **Meriden** — coaching template with newsletter + course product. Susan doesn't sell courses.
- **Anza** — too minimal, lacks specialty-prioritization structure.
- **Fletcher** — minimalist, but the structure is portfolio-style (not services + team).
- **Achromatic** — "achromatic" suggests black/white/grey palette; fights the warm target.
- **Looped** — community-feel, leans toward group/fitness, not solo practitioner.
- **Kusa, Lakshi, Maca, Saltless, Emmeline, Randi, Almar, Myhra, Altaloma, Alignflow, Colima, idrah, Florence** — not surveyed in depth; would need full audit pass. Strong candidates may emerge in [[template-audits]] follow-ups.

## Recommendation preview

**Primary recommendation: Clove** — purpose-built for mental health practice, structurally complete, language aligns with Susan's voice. Will need a small pass to:
- Replace group-practice content with solo-practitioner copy
- Add Susan's ASRM/PSI credential prominence (Clove already has team page with credentials, this fits)
- Swap out the "Book Consultation" CTA for "Email Susan" per SOW out-of-scope
- Apply WFC Calm Base palette (Clove's default color story may not match — needs visual review)

**Backup: Jenani** — if Clove's group-practice framing is too off-base for Susan, Jenani's solo-practitioner vibe + FAQ block is a clean substitute.
