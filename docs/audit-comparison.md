**The trade-off:**

- **Clove** wins on structural completeness, specialty fit, and the team-page pattern that maps directly to Susan's ASRM/PSI credential prominence. **But** the default color story may not match WFC's Calm Base (visual review needed), and the group-practice default copy needs ~70% rewrite.
- **Jenani** wins on solo-practitioner voice (built-in) and FAQ block (built-in). Slightly less direct on credential prominence but the "Pillars of care" framing translates to WFC's design-principles approach.

## Recommendation

### Primary: **Clove**

**Why:** Purpose-built for mental health practice. The team-page pattern with credentials (PSY.D, MD, PHD, ND, LAc) maps **directly** to Susan's ASRM/PSI credential prominence need — which is her single biggest differentiator per the WFC Fit Analysis. "Clove helped me find a therapist" testimonial pattern is exactly what Susan needs. "Providing you with a safe space to relax" is the language Susan should be using.

**What it needs:**
- 0.5–1.0 hr of copy rewrites (group → solo voice) — fits in Milestone 3 of the Phase 1 plan
- "BOOK A CONSULTATION" CTA → "Email Susan" link
- Visual review of the rendered template to confirm color story (or apply WFC Calm Base palette)
- Service architecture: 4 service cards (Clove's default) → 1 service with 11 specialty areas (Susan's structure)

### Backup: **Jenani**

If Susan finds the "Clove Therapy / mental health collective" framing too off-base for her solo practice, **Jenani** is a clean substitute. The solo-practitioner voice is already there, the FAQ block is built in (matches the SOW requirement), and the "Pillars of care" pattern translates directly to WFC's 6 design principles.

### Clune: do not recommend

Despite having the best color palette match, the spa-flavored copy ("Pamper your skin with products that are safe enough to eat") conflicts with WFC Principle 4 (Emotional Calibration). The entire copy flavor has to be replaced, which inflates adaptation cost to 1.5–2.0 hrs.

### Aurora: do not recommend

The e-commerce + retreat-booking infrastructure is wrong for Susan's "email-only intake per HIPAA" workflow. Removing it is 1.0–1.5 hrs of additional work, plus a full copy rewrite. Visually warm but the wrong shape.
