## 2. Engagement Summary

Susan Allen, LMFT is a solo-practice reproductive and perinatal mental health specialist serving San Francisco and Marin counties. Her current site is clean but minimally converting: broken `/services` page (404), no local search presence, dead blog, generic template that does not reflect the depth of her ASRM/PSI credentials. Consult intake currently arrives via email (referrals come directly to her inbox), and that workflow stays intact.

**The goals of this engagement, in order of priority:**

1. **Primary — a clean migration to the new site.** Move Susan onto an updated, well-structured template inside Squarespace; repair the broken services page; give her a repaired services page and clean up her template, while keeping her existing email-based consult intake workflow intact.
2. **Secondary — local findability.** Once the new site is live, ensure Susan is findable by intended parents, postpartum parents, and donors searching for credentialed specialists in SF and Marin — through traditional search (Google, Bing) and through generative AI engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews).

**Engagement model:** Susan stays on her existing platform (no platform migration). WFC works inside Squarespace to update the template, refresh the content with Lauren's clinical copy support, and lay a foundation that supports the secondary discoverability goal after Phase 1 lands. Consult intake stays in email per Susan's existing HIPAA-aware workflow.
