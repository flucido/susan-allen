Susan Allen is a solo-practice LMFT in San Francisco + Marin specializing in reproductive and perinatal mental health. Her current Squarespace site is clean but minimal: broken services page (404), no consult booking, no SEO, 3 blog posts in 10 months. She wants to stay on Squarespace — so this is a hybrid engagement: we help her work inside Squarespace with a template update, augmented by WFC's SEO/GEO, content strategy, and design guidance.
